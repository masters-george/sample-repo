Contact Screen Development

The contact screen is the heart of this project. You can search the databaase by pserson, company, or division. Think of a division as a separate location of the company.

To add, update, or delete an item, click on its pencil button. There is a pencil button for company, person, divisin, and note area. You can then update the content.
